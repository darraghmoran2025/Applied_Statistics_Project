1. Madan, Carr & Chang (1998)

Madan, D. B., Carr, P. P. and Chang, E. C. (1998). The Variance Gamma Process and Option Pricing. European Finance Review, 2(1), 79–105. DOI: 10.1023/A:1009703431535
Access
Free PDF (NYU hosted) DOI Journal (Oxford Academic)

The journal was renamed: European Finance Review is now Review of Finance, published by Oxford Academic. Either name is acceptable in a citation.

2. Cont (2001)

Cont, R. (2001). Empirical properties of asset returns: stylised facts and statistical issues. Quantitative Finance, 1(2), 223–236. DOI: 10.1080/713665670
Access
Free PDF (Author's site) DOI Journal (Taylor & Francis)

Author-hosted PDF on Cont's CNRS page. Over 8,600 citations as of 2026.

3. Christoffersen (1998)

Christoffersen, P. F. (1998). Evaluating Interval Forecasts. International Economic Review, 39(4), 841–862.
Access
Free PDF (UW-Madison) EconPapers / REPEC

No formal DOI. The companion code site contains R and MATLAB implementations of the UC, IND, and CC test statistics, useful for the Week 6 rolling backtest.
