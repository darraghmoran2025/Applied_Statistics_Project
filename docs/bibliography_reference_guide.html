Correction Applied
Barndorff-Nielsen was cited throughout as (1998). The correct year is 1997. The Finance and Stochastics paper carries a 1997 publication date. All in-text citations and the bibliography entry have been corrected in the updated literature review.

1. Madan, Carr & Chang (1998)

Madan, D. B., Carr, P. P. and Chang, E. C. (1998). The Variance Gamma Process and Option Pricing. European Finance Review, 2(1), 79–105. DOI: 10.1023/A:1009703431535
Access
Free PDF (NYU hosted) DOI Journal (Oxford Academic)

The journal was renamed: European Finance Review is now Review of Finance, published by Oxford Academic. Either name is acceptable in a citation.

2. Barndorff-Nielsen (1997, corrected from 1998)

Barndorff-Nielsen, O. E. (1997). Processes of Normal Inverse Gaussian Type. Finance and Stochastics, 2(1), 41–68. DOI: 10.1007/s007800050032
Access
Free PDF (SSRN preprint) DOI Journal (Springer)

The SSRN upload date of February 1998 is why the 1998 citation is common, but the Springer journal record shows 1997. Cross-check with your library catalogue before final submission.

3. Cont (2001)

Cont, R. (2001). Empirical properties of asset returns: stylised facts and statistical issues. Quantitative Finance, 1(2), 223–236. DOI: 10.1080/713665670
Access
Free PDF (Author's site) DOI Journal (Taylor & Francis)

Author-hosted PDF on Cont's CNRS page. Over 8,600 citations as of 2026.

4. Christoffersen (1998)

Christoffersen, P. F. (1998). Evaluating Interval Forecasts. International Economic Review, 39(4), 841–862.
Access
Free PDF (UW-Madison) EconPapers / REPEC

No formal DOI. The companion code site contains R and MATLAB implementations of the UC, IND, and CC test statistics, useful for the Week 6 rolling backtest.

5. Kupiec (1995)

Kupiec, P. H. (1995). Techniques for Verifying the Accuracy of Risk Measurement Models. Journal of Derivatives, 3(2), 73–84. DOI: 10.3905/jod.1995.407942
Access
Risk.net (Journal of Derivatives) DOI

Introduces the proportions of failures (POF) likelihood ratio test for unconditional VaR coverage, commonly called the Kupiec test. Christoffersen (1998) extends this by adding the conditional independence test. Both are applied in the Week 6 rolling backtest.

6. Vehtari, Gelman & Gabry (2017)

Vehtari, A., Gelman, A. and Gabry, J. (2017). Practical Bayesian model evaluation using leave-one-out cross-validation and WAIC. Statistics and Computing, 27(5), 1413–1432. DOI: 10.1007/s11222-016-9696-4
Access
Free PDF (arXiv:1507.04544) DOI Journal (Springer) loo R Package (Stan)

arXiv preprint is essentially identical to the published version. A published erratum exists at DOI 10.1007/s11222-016-9709-3; the substance of the paper is unchanged.
